Food Pantry Resources

Welcome to the Food Pantry

The University of Michigan offers resources to help students access nutritious food and ensure no one goes hungry. Below are resources and services available to you.

Maize and Blue Cupboard

The Maize and Blue Cupboard provides students with access to fresh produce, non-perishable items, and cooking tools to support healthy eating habits.

Community Food Services

Partner organizations across Ann Arbor collaborate with the university to provide meal programs and grocery assistance for students in need.

How to Access Food Pantry Services

  1. Register online through the official Maize and Blue Cupboard website.
  2. Visit the pantry during operating hours with your student ID.
  3. Contact the pantry staff for questions or dietary accommodations.
